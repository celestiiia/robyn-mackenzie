the project;


This project was one of our final projects in the first year of my graphic design degree. We visited the Hauser & Wirth Bruton Gallery and had to choose one of 3 briefs to address. Despite being one of the most diverse galleries, Bruton especially did not express enough about the artists from minority backgrounds to us as visitors.


Our concept was a cultural program culminating in an exhibition to try and tackle the problems we found within the art world upon further research, centring H&W as the flagship gallery for this revolution.
