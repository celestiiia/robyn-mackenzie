About

I'm Robyn, a Dorset-based designer and user researcher currently completing a BA in Graphic Design at Arts University Bournemouth.


I work as a freelance content designer with a focus on accessibility and usability — I'm interested in how plain language and clear structure can make services genuinely work for the people who use them.


Previously I supported digital transformation projects at an NHS trust, helping teams run UAT, carry out functional testing, and manage the day-to-day of complex service change.


Outside of work: Dorset coastlines, borderline-baked cookies, parkrun volunteering, rollerskating, and a lot of noodles.
